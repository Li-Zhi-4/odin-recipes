Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Chewy, homemade peanut butter cookies.
This makes 24 cookies for one batch and takes approximately 15 minutes to prepare.

Cookie Dough Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl together in a bowl. If the mixture seems dry, add a few tablespoons of canola or grapeseed oil.
  2. Roll the mixture into little balls and place on an ungreased cookie sheet.
  3. Wet the bottom of a glass with water and dip in sugar. Press cookies down into a circular shape with glass. Sprinkle additional sugar if needed.
  4. Make a cross hatch pattern with a fork across the flattened cookies.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees Celsius for 7-10 minutes.
  6. Cool on parchment paper.
